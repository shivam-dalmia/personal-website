01. About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! I'm Shivam Dalmia, a Computer Science and Statistics graduate from Rice University with a passion for building technology that solves real-world problems.

My journey began at South City International School in Kolkata, where serving as School Captain and varsity team captain helped shape me into a confident leader and collaborative teammate. In 2021, I took the leap to Rice to pursue Computer Science, where I grew not just academically through rigorous coursework, research, and hands-on projects, but also socially, learning how to lead diverse teams, communicate ideas clearly, and thrive in fast-paced environments.

Through internships and research, I've developed strong skills in software engineering, data-driven problem solving, and cross-functional collaboration, working closely with designers, engineers, and stakeholders to turn ideas into impactful products. Beyond tech, you'll usually find me on the golf course or tennis court, playing guitar, traveling to new places, or spending time with friends—experiences that keep me grounded, creative, and energized.
